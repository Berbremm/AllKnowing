The Orchid Child Ján Fidrman
Catalogue

Ever concerned with equality, Fidrman decided early on to combine his rejection of society with social rejection. Born in Slovakia, Fidrman soon started hating shit music that sounds good, dreaming of composing good music that sounds shit. He organised his personal life similarly, deciding to prepare for a hobo life of inactivity by gaining a bachelor degree in Chinese. After a brief stint as a wedding musician in China, Fidrman returned to Holland with grand aspirations, joining the Rotterdam label Reckless Records in 2020 and releasing the single 'Hobo Story'. Following the release, a series of hilarities inspired him to begrudgingly write his first EP, 'Hobo Love'. An instant masterpiece, Fidrman uses a perfect mix of wisdom and idiocy to highlight the world’s problems while comforting his public with the idea that they are too stupid to solve anything.

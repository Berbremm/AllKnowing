Bio

Born and raised in the south of Brazil, Bernardo Bremm, a.k.a The Orchid Child, is a music producer/visual artist based in Rotterdam. Coming from a highly eclectic musical background, The Orchid Child fluently navigates throughout styles and genres, while simultaneously adapting to them his own signature.

Bernardo started his musical education with piano lessons during high-school. Soon after his graduation, he moved to Holland intending to obtain a bachelor’s degree in music. Although succeeding at the conservatory audition, he refused the offer and enrolled in a cultural studies programme instead. Incessantly determined to pursue a musical carrer regardless of possessing a diploma or not, Bernardo moved to Rotterdam in the beginning of 2020, subsequently assembling the projects The Orchid Child and Killercat Records.
